Admissions

Our admissions team supports every applicant with tailored guidance to ensure a smooth journey into Rivermark University's vibrant academic community.

Start Your Application

Application Process

  1. Apply Online: Submit the digital application and select your programs of interest.
  2. Prepare Documents: Upload transcripts, test scores, statements, and recommendations.
  3. Interview: Participate in a virtual conversation with admissions or faculty.
  4. Offer: Receive a decision notification and financial aid package.
  5. Enrollment: Confirm attendance, register for classes, and attend orientation.

Scholarships & Funding

Merit-based Scholarships

Automatic consideration for applicants with outstanding academic and leadership records. Awards range from $4,000 to $12,000 annually.

Need-based Grants

Designed for students demonstrating financial need. Submit the Rivermark Aid Profile to be considered for grants and tuition waivers.

Research Assistantships

Graduate students may secure assistantships through research centers, providing stipends, tuition coverage, and mentorship.
